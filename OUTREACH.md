# Outreach System v1

## Niche
B2B SaaS: devtools, fintech infra, vertical SaaS.

## Message angle
"You don't need more AI tools. You need an accountable content operating system."

## Cold DM template
Hey {{first_name}} — quick idea:

Most teams are testing AI content, but almost none have a reliable operating system for it.

I run RelayRank, where we deploy a managed Paperclip workflow that ships SEO content weekly (with approval gates + audit trail + KPI reporting).

If useful, I can send a 1-page teardown of your current organic pipeline and a 30-day sprint plan.

## Teardown offer CTA
"Want me to send your teardown by tomorrow?"

## Discovery call agenda (20 min)
1. Current inbound baseline
2. Content bottlenecks
3. Existing tooling/workflows
4. 30-day sprint plan
5. Pilot scope + timeline

## Qualification checklist
- Has clear ICP
- Publishes or wants to publish regularly
- Can approve weekly content
- Cares about pipeline, not just traffic
